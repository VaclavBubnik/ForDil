Privacy Policy

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

The application request access to your device location to enable GPS functionality and display your current position on the map. This location data is used only locally on your device and is not stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties.

Location access is optional and can be denied or revoked at any time through your device settings.
